I wrote my thesis at the research group of Prof. V. Boeva in the department of computer science of ETH Zurich. LAUNCH 2021

In the course of 2021 I joined the ETH Entrepreneur Club to help the organization team of “Launch by ETH Entrepreneur Club", a new really cool event in Zurich. The goal of Launch is to bring the startup ecosystem of Zurich together for a one night event, with talks, workshops and booths. The first edition had a huge success with 1000+ participant, and the second edition in 2022 was even greater.
